PGDAV COLLEGE [DU]

PGDAV College is one of the oldest constituents of the University of Delhi. It was established in 1957 with the vision of the Dayanand Anglo Vedic movement (DAV) for imparting world class education rooted in the Indian milieu.

Visit the Official College Website

Courses in Our College

The College offers 13 undergraduate programmes taught by 12 departments in the disciplines of Arts, Commerce, Computer Science and Mathematical Sciences. The College also offers postgraduate courses in Commerce, Hindi, Mathematics and Political Science.

Commerce

The Department offers two Undergraduate Degree Courses in Commerce, namely B.Com. and B.Com. (Hons). It also offers one Postgraduate Course, M.Com. Links to B.Com (Hons.) and B.Com.

Economics

The Department offers B.A. (Hons) Economics. After pursuing this Honours programme, the students will be well equipped to enter a postgraduate programme in any Indian university or a university outside

Computer Science

The Department offers B.Sc. (Hons) Computer Science, BA (Programme) Computer Applications and provides Computer Science as one of the disciplines in B.Sc. Mathematical Sciences.

Mathematics

The Department offers B.A. (Hons) Mathematics and offers the Mathematics as discipline in BA Programme and B.SC. Mathematical Sciences. Link to B.A. (Hons) Mathematics.

Hindi

The Department offers B.Sc. (Hons) Statistics and offers Statistics as discipline in B.Sc. Mathematical Sciences.

English

The Department offers B.A. (Hons) English and offers English in BA Programme.

Our College Campus

P.G.D.A.V. College holds the pride of being one of the most beautiful campuses of Delhi University. It is one of the largest constituent colleges of the University. The campus is strategically located on the Ring Road, near Vinobapuri metro station.

Our Facilities

The College meets the latest infrastructural requirements. The Campus is Wi-Fi enabled and there is a fully functional Computer Hub for students to access the internet and various Delhi University Library System resources. The College has added smart classrooms equipped with overhead projectors and separate departmental rooms. It has a grand auditorium and state-of-the-art seminar halls. A lift facility for the differently-abled students is also provided. The College has a branch of the Central Bank of India. The College has an RO water system. The Canteen is one of the largest in the University, serving a variety of cuisines. The College also has a photostat kiosk. There is a well equipped fitness centre. Medical facilities with an attending doctor are also available to the students. In our endeavour to promote ecological sustenance, the campus is fully equipped with a rainwater harvesting system, beautiful lawns and herbal gardens.

Seminar Halls

The College takes pride in having two very well equipped, state-of-the-art, fully air-conditioned and acoustically well-maintained Seminar Halls for academic and cultural activities. They are the venues for lectures, debates, discussions, and workshops and other such academic and cultural activities.

About Our College

PGDAV College is one of the oldest constituents of the University of Delhi. It was established in 1957 with the vision of the Dayanand Anglo Vedic movement (DAV) for imparting world class education rooted in the Indian milieu. The College presents a bouquet of thirteen vibrant undergraduate and four postgraduate courses across twelve disciplines where it offers both the Honours and Programme degrees at the undergraduate level. We have a team of committed and highly qualified teaching faculty and supporting staff. Along with rigorous academic and intellectual training, the College also provides co-curricular and cultural activities for the students. Our wards excel in sports, fine arts, music, dance, and theatre. The College consistently receives numerous accolades in sports and has nurtured and groomed students to achieve distinctions at national and international levels.
